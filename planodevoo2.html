Audiovisual experiment

Plano de Voo II

(Flight Plan II)

Posteriorly exploring one of Flight Plan's topics on the idea of a lost childhood and the premature death and kills of poor children in the favelas, the last work for the discipline evolved into a video production with the originally inspired song. The video is a compilation of brazilian's movies footage on the theme, visually edited and set in a series of experimentations with transparencies, sobrepositions, fast continuous cuts and symbolic elements shown in parallel with remarkable images of violence in these places.
