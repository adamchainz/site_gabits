About Me


Gabriela M Valle

Artist and Web Developer

Extremely communicative, easily motivated and quite humorous, I’m a very ethusiastic girl who is passionate about art, architecture and history, intimately concerned about social matters and the social structures, dedicated to explore aesthetics and analyze the intuitive perception of visual stimulus.

Being curious but very dissatisfied with our current educational system, I do believe in taking pleasure from studying, specially through the internet, and that's how I've been teaching myself most of what I know. I have a constantly thinking but also quite creative mind, for besides being a daydreamer and story-teller, it’s usually a personal impulse to find solutions for problems that don’t even exist yet.
